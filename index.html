Application Guide Practical guide for using Essam Procurement Solution as a procurement follow-up system. Beta 4.4 × Overview Workflow Tasks Meetings Contacts Manager Tips Feedback What this application is for This app is designed for procurement follow-up: tasks, projects, meetings, notes, contacts, reports, and daily priorities in one place. The main goal is not to make your team write long reports. The goal is to capture the minimum useful information so you can see what is pending, who is responsible, and what needs action today. Best rule: every important follow-up should become a task linked to a project, owner, priority, and due date. What to use it for Daily follow-up See today’s urgent work and pending actions. Project control Keep each task connected to the right project. Team assignment Know who is handling each procurement item. Meeting tracking Record meetings and convert decisions into tasks. Supplier contacts Keep supplier details and specialties searchable. Reports Review workload, status, and overdue actions quickly.
